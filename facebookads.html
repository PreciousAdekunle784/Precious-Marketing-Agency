Why Your Meta Ads Are Failing

We Have Audited Hundreds
Of Meta Accounts. They All
Have the Same Problems.

The platform is not broken. The setup is. Facebook and Instagram Ads can generate extraordinary returns in virtually every industry when they are structured correctly, managed actively, and tested systematically. Here is why yours are not.

🏷
You Are Boosting Posts Instead of Running Campaigns. That Is Not the Same Thing.
Boosting a post gives Meta a budget and asks it to spend it. It does. Mostly on people who scroll past, vaguely like the content, and will never buy. A real campaign starts with a defined objective, a specific audience, a dedicated landing page, and a conversion event Meta can actually optimise against. Boosted posts have none of those things. You are paying for reach you cannot convert.
🎬
Your Creative Stopped Working Three Weeks Ago. Nobody Noticed.
Meta audiences experience creative fatigue faster than any other platform. The same image shown to the same person five times generates irritation, not action. In accounts without active creative testing, CPL typically doubles within 45 days of launch as audiences exhaust. Most agencies run the same ads for months. We rotate tested winners every two to three weeks before fatigue kills performance.
📈
Your Pixel Is Firing. But It Is Not Tracking What Actually Matters.
A pixel that fires on page loads is not the same as a pixel tracking real conversions. Without verified purchase, lead, or booking events, Meta's algorithm optimises for the wrong outcomes. It will find people who click, not people who buy. In our audits, fewer than 30% of Meta accounts have their conversion events set up and verified correctly. In every account we inherit where performance has mysteriously declined, misconfigured events are the root cause. We verify every event before spending a dollar.
🙋
You Are Running One Campaign to a Cold Audience and Wondering Why It Does Not Convert.
A cold audience does not know you, does not trust you, and is not ready to buy on first exposure. Running a single conversion campaign to strangers and expecting direct sales is like proposing marriage on the first date. Profitable Meta Ads require a three-layer structure: cold for awareness, warm for nurturing, retargeting for conversion. Without all three layers active simultaneously, you are leaving most of your potential revenue unaddressed.
