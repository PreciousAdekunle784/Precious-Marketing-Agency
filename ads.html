The Same Four Problems.

We know exactly why your campaigns are not working. Not because we are guessing. Because we have seen the same broken patterns in account after account, industry after industry. Here is what is costing you money right now, almost certainly.

💵
Right Now, Nearly Half Your Budget Is Going to People Who Will Never Become Customers
Broad match keywords mean Google decides what searches to show your ad on. And Google's interest is in spending your budget, not protecting your margins. The result: job seekers, students, researchers, competitors. In the last 50 accounts we audited, the average wasted spend was 52% of total budget. You are not paying for leads. You are paying for traffic that has a zero percent chance of converting. We cut this in the first week.
🚨
The Ad Worked. The Visitor Arrived. Then Your Landing Page Sent Them to Your Competitor.
The click cost you money. What happened next cost you the sale. Most landing pages have no single clear offer, no reason to act now, and four or five things competing for the visitor's attention at once. Studies across thousands of campaigns show 70% or more of ad value is lost at the landing page, not the ad. You can write perfect ads and still haemorrhage budget if the page is weak. We fix the page before we scale the traffic. Always.
📈
Your Tracking Is Almost Certainly Broken. Which Means Google Is Optimising Against You.
When Google's algorithm cannot see your real conversions, it optimises for proxy signals: clicks, page views, time on site. Not leads. Not sales. Not revenue. In our audits, fewer than 30% of accounts have conversion tracking set up correctly. That means 70% are essentially paying Google to optimise for the wrong goal. Every dollar spent in that state is a dollar helping Google learn how to waste your next dollar more efficiently. Tracking is the first thing we fix. Before any spend. Every time.
👻
Your Agency Reviews Your Account Once a Month. The Auction? It Moves Every Hour.
Google Ads is not a set-it-and-forget-it channel. Competitor bids shift overnight. New search terms emerge daily. Ad fatigue sets in within weeks. An account that is not actively managed deteriorates. The campaigns that look good in month one and terrible by month four were not touched in between. We are inside every account we manage every single business day. Search terms reviewed. Bids adjusted. Losers paused. Winners scaled. That discipline is the entire difference between a 1.2x ROAS and a 6.8x ROAS.
